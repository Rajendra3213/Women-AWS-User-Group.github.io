Mission

Our mission is to create a robust support system that touches the lives of thousands of women, providing a safe, encouraging environment where they can excel exponentially in the field of technology.
  • To train, educate, and empower women in technology across Nepal, fostering a supportive community with the support from Amazon Web Services (AWS) and its global network of community members.
  • Through collaborative efforts, we strive to bring together expert individuals, both men and women, to encourage and uplift their fellow women colleagues and friends.

Vision

To be the forefront catalyst for positive transformation in Nepal's tech landscape, mirroring the global initiatives of AWS.

We envision a future where gender equality, diversity, and inclusion are integral to the essence, structure, and composition of the technology industry in Nepal. Our vision extends beyond conventional boundaries, aspiring to establish an ecosystem where women not only enter but thrive and excel in technology. AWS Women User Group Nepal aims to contribute to a more inclusive and innovative future, breaking down barriers and transforming challenges into opportunities. Together, we work towards a brighter, more equitable future for women in technology in Nepal.
