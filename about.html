About Us

  1. Home
  2. About Us

Welcome to AWS User Group Women In Tech Nepal! We're a vibrant community-driven platform powered by Amazon Web Services (AWS).

Our community brings together women and men from the tech world, especially in cloud computing, right here in the beautiful landscape of Nepal. Our goal is to create a welcoming space especially for underprivileged and underrepresented women where we help to uplift their learning, skills and confidence across the nation through our different initiatives. At AWS User Group Women In Tech Nepal, we believe in the power of collaboration, diversity, and inclusion. Our community thrives on the shared passion for technology, and we are committed to fostering an environment where both women and men in tech can come together to learn, grow, and create meaningful connections.

Come join us as we simplify tech talk, share experiences, and together, make the world of technology more accessible, enjoyable, and authentic for everyone!
